GOOGLE UX COURSE OUTLINE

COURSE CONTENT/ LESSON PLAN

Course Title: Certified PMP Professional

Duration: 3 Months

Trainer Name:
Week 1
Introduction
Day 1 Introduction to UX design:What is UX? Why is it important? Key principles, practices
Day 2 Understanding Users:Basics of user research; how to collect data about users (interviews, surveys etc.)
Day 3 Creating Personas :What are personas and why they are important; Steps to create detailed user personas
Day 4 User Stories and Scenarios: Writing up stories and creating scenarios; How these tools help in understanding user needs
Day 5 Competitive Analysis:Research the competition; Understand the needs and market.
Week 2
Basics of Wireframing
Day 1 Information Architecture:Organizing information effectively; Creating site maps and user flows.
Day 2 Basics of Wireframing Introduction to wireframing:Tools and techniques to create wireframes
Day 3 Advanced Wireframing:How to create detailed wireframes; best practice and common mistakes
Day 4 Prototyping Basics:Introduce prototyping, differences between low-fidelity and high-fidelity prototypes
Day 5 Interactive Prototyping:Design interactive prototypes through popular tools like Figma or Sketch.
Week 3
Principles of UX Design
Day 1 Basics of User Interface Design:Principles of user interface design; Elements of user interface design (buttons, icons, etc.)
Day 2 Visual Design Principles:Composition and balance; Visual hierarchy; Effective use of grids and layouts.
Day 3 Typography in UX Design:Choice of appropriate fonts; laws and principles of Typography; Accessible Typography
Day 4 Color Theory in UX Design:The basics of color theory; Applying color in design; Color psychology.
Day 5 Design Systems and Style Guides Creating Design Systems:Developing a consitent set of design standards and style guides
Week 4
Base of Interaction Design
Day 1 Introduction to the Base of Interaction Design:Understanding Interaction Design; Principles of Creating Interactive Elements
Day 2 Microinteractions:Designing Small Interactive Elements to Enhance User Experience.
Day 3 Mobile UX Design:Best practices in designing mobile user experiences.
Day 4 Responsive Design:Creating designs that work on various devices and screen sizes
Day 5 Accessibility in User Experience Design:Making your designs accessible to everyone, including those with disabilities.
Week 5
Prototyping Techniques
Day 1 Advanced Prototyping Techniques:How to use advanced features in Prototyping Tools like Figma or Sketch.
Day 2 Design for Different Platforms:Understand the difference in designing on in the web and on mobile.
Day 3 Usability Testing with Users:How to Conduct Effective Usability Tests; Methods and Tools.
Day 4 Usability Test Results:Analysis Interpretation of results and areas for improvement.
Day 5 Iterative Design ProcessGive iterations of designs based on user feedback and results of the testing process.
Week 6
Analytics
Day 1 UX Metrics and Analytics:Measurement of the success of UX design by different metrics and analytics tools.
Day 2 Project Management for User Experience:Managing UX projects; Tools and techniques for effective project management.
Day 3 Collaboration in UX teams:Stakeholder and cross-functional team collaboration.
Day 4 Agile UX:Integrating UX design processes into Agile methodologies.
Day 5 Presenting UX Work:Best practices for presenting UX designs and findings to stakeholders.
Week 7
Content
Day 1 UX Writing:The writing of effective and user-friendly UX copy.
Day 2 Content Strategy in UX:design a content strategy that correlates with UX goals.
Day 3 Design Patterns:Understanding common design patterns in UX and using them effectively.
Day 4 Service Design:End-to-end design of the user experience and service blueprints.
Day 5 Ethics in UX Design: Understanding ethical considerations and designing responsibly.
Week 8
Global UX Design
Day 1 Global UX Design:Design for Universal Audience; Take into Account Cultural Differences.
Day 2 Localization in UX Design: Design adaptation for languages, and cultures TARGET.
Day 3 Psychology in UX Design:Understanding the psychology behind user behaviour and decision-making.
Day 4 Storytelling in UX:Making Use of story telling techniques while enhancing.
Day 5 Future Trends in UX:Tapping into the emerging trends and future directions in UX design.
Week 9
Portfolio
Day 1 How to Build a UX Portfolio:All You Need to Know When It Comes to the Creation and Presentation of a Compelling UX Portfolio.
Day 2 Careers in User Experience Design:It talks about the various jobs roles and several career paths possible in UX.
Day 3 Networking in UX:Building a professional network within the UX community.
Day 4 Preparing for UX Interviews:Tips and best practices for acing UX job interviews.
Day 5 Mock Interviews:Participating in mock interviews to gain confidence and feedback.
Week 10
Advanced UI Design
Day 1 Advanced UI DesignThis module covers the most advanced principles and techniques of user interface design.
Day 2 Designing for Different User Requirements:Covering different user needs regarding accessibility, inclusivity, and diversity..
Day 3 A/B Testing: How to understand and run A/B testing for the improvement of UX designs.
Day 4 Heuristics Evaluation:In light of this, learn to perform heuristic evaluations to help in the identification of usability issues.
Day 5 Gathering and using user feedback for continuous design:improvement is a part of user feedback and continuous improvement.
Week 11
Case studies
Day 1 Case studies on UX design:Study and analysis of case studies in the real life of user experience design.
Day 2 Data-driven design:A designer makes up their mind on UX through data and analytics..
Day 3 Designing for Emerging Technologies:UX design considerations for making experiences for new and emerging technologies, such as AR/VR..
Day 4 Transcultural Design:To understand and practice discerning the principles of transcultural design.
Day 5 Sustainable UX Design:A set of principles referring to designing for our environment, designing digital products which are environmentally friendly, and sustainable.
Week 12
Final Project
Day 1 Final Project Planning:Plan and outline the final project; define project goals and deliverables.
Day 2 Final Project Workday :1 Work on Final Project.
Day 3 Final Project Workday 2:Continue working on the final project: Refine designs and prototypes.
Day 4 Final Project Review:Final presentations to the class for feedback and critique.
Day 5 Course Wrap-Up and Next Step: Review the key learnings; Next steps and career opportunities in UX design.
